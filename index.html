VKCrypto

VKCrypto by MK - new Client for VK social network, which allows you keep your correspondence secret. It using RSA-4096+Camellia-256 Cyphers, so you can be absolutely quiet about your messages. The application supports two-factor authentication. In addition to sending messages, you can also share images with your friends. The images are also securely encrypted. The app is under active development.
